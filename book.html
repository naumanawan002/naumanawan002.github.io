To Kill a Mockingbird

By Harper Lee

Summary

To Kill a Mockingbird is a novel by Harper Lee published in 1960. It is set in Maycomb, Alabama, during the Great Depression, and follows the story of a young girl named Scout Finch as she grows up and learns about the harsh realities of racism and prejudice in her community.

Why I Enjoyed This Book

  • The story is engaging and well-written.
  • The characters are complex and memorable.
  • The book deals with important themes that are still relevant today.

Publication Date

July 11, 1960
